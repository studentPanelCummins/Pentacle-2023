General Instructions:

1. All teams must register and complete payments before the date of the event (Installations not permitted).

2. No registrations will be accepted on the spot.

3. Payments can be made in cash or via Google pay.

4. Payment receipts must be produced at the reporting desk.

5. Documents (applicable to all team members) required for verification:

  • Bonafide letter - on the official college letterhead along with college stamp (Original Hardcopy).
  • College ID cards or library card.
  • Current year Fee Receipt. (Original Hardcopy)

    • 6. Teams are required to report to the specified venue at least 30 minutes prior to their match.

    7. Campus teams not allowed. All players in the team must be from the same college.

    8. The organizers reserve the right to disqualify the entire team for the tournament in case of producing fake documents and fake players. Year down (Y.D.) students are not permitted to play.

    9. In case an objection is raised by any team, a written application along with a protest of 500/- needs to be submitted. The age limit is 23 years.

    Guidelines:

    1. A team can have a maximum of 12 players.

    2. Each quarter will be of 7 minutes running (girls) / 7 minutes running (boys) up to quarter-finals. Semi finals and final will have a quarter of 10 mins running for both categories.

    3. In case of a tie, 5 minutes of extra time will be allotted.

    4. Playing kit is compulsory for each participant.

    5. Jersey numbers are compulsory for team members.

    6. Hitting the opponents or fighting will be given a technical.

    7. The decision made by the referees will be final.
