General Instructions

1. All teams must register and complete payments before the date of the event (Installations not permitted).

2. No registrations will be accepted on the spot.

3. Payments can be made in cash or via Google pay.

4. Payment receipts must be produced at the reporting desk.

5. Documents (applicable to all team members) required for verification:

  • Bonafide letter - on the official college letterhead along with college stamp (Original Hardcopy).
  • College ID cards or library card.
  • Current year Fee Receipt. (Original Hardcopy)

    • 6. Teams are required to report to the specified venue at least 30 minutes prior to their match.

    7. Campus teams not allowed. All players in the team must be from the same college.

    8. The organizers reserve the right to disqualify the entire team for the tournament in case of producing fake documents and fake players. Year down (Y.D.) students are not permitted to play.

    9. In case an objection is raised by any team, a written application along with a protest of 500/- needs to be submitted. The age limit is 23 years.

    Guidelines:

    1. Each team must consist of 7 players and 5 substitutes.

    2. Each half will be of 10 minutes and the match will be played in 2 equal halves. Semi finals will have each half of 12 minutes and final will have each half of 15 minutes

    3. Rolling substitutes.

    4. Players will not be permitted to play without shin guards, studs and stockings during the match.

    5. In case of a draw, there will be penalties (3 for each team)

    6. For corners, the players who don't have ball possession should stand 5 steps away from the ball.

    7. Outside should be taken as kick-ins within 3 seconds after the ball is placed on the line. Otherwise, opponents will get the possession and the opponents should stand 3 steps away from the ball.

    8. Direct goals from outsides are not allowed.

    9. Direct goals from center spot are allowed.

    10. Keeper can only throw underarm after receiving the ball by hand. He/she can't pick the ball if it is a back pass by his/ her own team player and if he/she does so, it will result in an indirect kick for the opponent.

    11. Sliding tackles done by any players playing the match will be penalised with a free kick.

    12. Yellow and red card rules will be according to the FIFA rules.

    13. Abusive language by any players during the match will result in a red card which will be applicable for that match itself.

    14. Hitting the opponents or fighting will be given a red card.

    15. Extra time for the match will be decided by the referees to compensate the stoppage/injury time.

    16. Strict action will be taken against anybody who verbally abuses the referees. Decision made by the referees will be final.

    17. Jersey numbers are compulsory for all team members.
